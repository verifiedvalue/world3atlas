Atlas key

How the orb encodes each realm—readiness on color, difficulty on the coin, border policy in the scroll.

Readiness hue

Country fills and realm-token glow follow realm readiness: the average of affluence, security, safety, quality of life, and language ease (same five bars in the briefing). Cooler reds mark rougher footing; greens mark stronger overall footing. Tokens keep a gold filigree ring; the core shifts with readiness.

Token icon · difficulty

The glyph inside each realm coin is the expedition tier—how punishing the authored difficulty curve is for heroes, separate from readiness hue.

🌱 Harbor Calm 🧭 Caravan Road 🛡️ Citadel Approach 🔥 Iron Pass 💀 Apex March ⚔️ Contested Ground ☣️ Blight March

Contested Ground and Blight March override the usual ladder when safety collapses into extreme hazard brackets.

Border access

Policy posture for entry—shown in tooltips and realm scrolls. These chips are not the map gradient; they summarize visa friction beside readiness.

Open — welcoming pathways; lighter paperwork friction.
Restricted — selective long-stay and work routes.
Locked — very limited ordinary entry; expect gates.
Fortress — hardest border class; plan entry like a citadel siege.
